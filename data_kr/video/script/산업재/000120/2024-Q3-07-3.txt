The orientation for the seventh round
of CJ's open innovation program O!VentUs
was held on June 26.
The eight startup selected this year
will work for around four months
with CJ CheilJedang, CJ Logistics, and CJ ENM
to pursue
a health manangement platform,
AI-powered worksite automtion solution,
and other innovative projects.
The participating startups this year
will also receive professional consulting
on corporate PR and IR
and benefit from growth programs
such as CJ Connect Day, which examines
the possibility
of investment and business articulations.
