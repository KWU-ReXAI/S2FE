- **Drone Delivery Model**: The core of the delivery model targets urban areas with collective buildings. - **Safety and Efficiency**: The system includes a ground control system for setup and monitoring, ensuring efficient and safe navigation. In case of emergencies, such as strong winds, the drone can autonomously deploy a parachute within one second to prevent crashes and protect customer goods.

- **Emergency Protocols**: In the event of an emergency landing, both the drone and customer goods are safely returned to the logistics center via a mobile control station, ensuring minimal disruption and damage.
