**Summary of Key Economic Ideas and Arguments:**

1. **Current Market Situation:**
   - The KOSPI index has reached its highest point of the year but is experiencing a weak upward trend.
   - The market atmosphere is subdued due to prolonged economic downturns and the failed IPO of Hotel Lotte.

2. **Upcoming Major IPOs:**
   - Three major companies are set to go public, potentially reversing the current market sentiment:
     - **Samsung Biologics:** 
       - Expected IPO proceeds: 3 trillion KRW.
       - Anticipated market capitalization post-IPO: 10 trillion KRW.
       - Attracts interest as a high-growth industry less affected by economic cycles, offering a solid investment alternative.
     - **Doosan Bobcat:**
       - Acquired by Doosan in 2007, it is the world’s leading small construction equipment company.
       - Target IPO in mid-October.
       - Expected IPO proceeds: 1 trillion KRW.
       - Anticipated market capitalization: up to 5 trillion KRW.
     - **Netmarble:**
       - Chose KOSPI over KOSDAQ and NASDAQ.
       - Plans to complete IPO by February next year.
       - Expected market capitalization: 10 trillion KRW.

3. **Economic Impact:**
   - The combined expected IPO proceeds of these three companies could reach up to 6 trillion KRW, surpassing last year’s total KOSPI market IPO proceeds of 3.5 trillion KRW.
   - These IPOs and the influx of capital are expected to provide momentum for KOSPI to break out of its current stagnation.

- Introduction of the current market condition and the term "Boxpi" due to the stagnant KOSPI index.
- Presentation of the upcoming IPOs as potential game-changers for the market.
