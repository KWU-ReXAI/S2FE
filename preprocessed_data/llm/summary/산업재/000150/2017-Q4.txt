**Summary of Key Economic Ideas and Developments:**

- **Doosan Heavy Industries' Expansion into Vietnam's Wind Power Market:**
  - Doosan Heavy Industries has entered the Vietnamese wind power market, marking a significant step in its international expansion strategy.
  - The company has signed an agreement with Vietnam Electricity (EVN) to construct a 3MW offshore wind power demonstration complex, which will be integrated with an energy storage system (ESS).

- **Partnerships and Agreements:**
  - Doosan has also formed a business agreement with CC1, a comprehensive construction company in Vietnam, to develop a wind power complex. CC1 holds the rights to a 105MW wind power project, indicating a substantial scale of development.

- **Strategic Economic Diplomacy:**
  - Park Ji-won, the chairman of Doosan Heavy Industries, is currently in Vietnam as part of the economic delegation accompanying South Korean President Moon Jae-in. This highlights the role of economic diplomacy in facilitating international business ventures.
