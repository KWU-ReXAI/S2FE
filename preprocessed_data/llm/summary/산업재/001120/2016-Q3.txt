**Summary of Key Economic Ideas and Arguments:**

- **Investment and Expansion:**
  - LG Chem plans to invest 114 billion won by 2018 to expand its synthetic resin plant in Huizhou, Guangdong Province, China.
  - The expansion aims to double the plant's annual production capacity from 150,000 tons to 300,000 tons.

- **Product Characteristics and Applications:**
  - The synthetic resin is a type of synthetic rubber known for its resistance to heat and impact, as well as its ease of processing.
  - It is widely used in the automotive and home appliance industries.

- **Market Impact:**
  - Upon completion of the plant expansion, LG Chem expects its global market share to increase from 21% to 26%.

**Logical Structure:**

1. **Introduction of Investment Plan:**
   - Announcement of LG Chem's significant financial commitment to expand production capacity in China.

2. **Details of the Product:**
   - Description of the synthetic resin's properties and its industrial applications.

3. **Expected Outcomes:**
   - Anticipated increase in production capacity and market share as a result of the expansion.
