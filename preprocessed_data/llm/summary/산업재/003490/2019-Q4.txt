**Summary of Key Economic Ideas and Actions:**

- **Salary Reductions:**
  - Daehan Airlines announced that executives at the vice president level and above will return 50% of their salaries.
  - Senior executives will return 40% of their salaries, and managing directors will return 30%.

- **Asset Liquidation:**
  - Daehan Airlines plans to sell idle assets, such as the Songhyeon-dong site, to raise funds.

- **Cost-Cutting Measures:**
  - Asiana Airlines, which declared emergency management last month, will increase unpaid leave next month.
  - The airline will operate with half of its workforce and executives will return 60% of their salaries.

These measures reflect efforts by both airlines to stabilize their financial situations amid economic challenges.
