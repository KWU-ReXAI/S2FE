**Summary of Key Economic Ideas and Arguments:**

- **Third-Party Allotment of New Shares:**
  - LX Marine Solution has decided to issue new shares through a third-party allotment to LX Cable.
  - This type of capital increase is generally considered a positive development, especially when it is aimed at expanding production facilities and investments rather than merely securing operational funds.

- **Significance of the Allotment:**
  - The allotment was executed on May 24, with LX Cable, the largest shareholder, as the recipient.
  - The capital raised amounts to approximately 30 to 35 billion won.

- **Implications of the Largest Shareholder's Involvement:**
  - The involvement of the largest shareholder in such an allotment is seen as a strong indicator of confidence in the industry’s prospects.
  - Historically, when the largest shareholder participates in a third-party allotment, it rarely results in failure.
  - This move is not just a defensive strategy to support stock prices but a demonstration of confidence in the business environment.

- **Market Perception:**
  - The participation of the largest shareholder can be interpreted as forming a solid support line for the company’s stock, reflecting a positive outlook on the company’s future performance.
