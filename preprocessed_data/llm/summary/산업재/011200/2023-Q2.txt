  - Concerns about the "winner's curse" are also present, where the successful bidder might overpay and face difficulties post-acquisition.

- **Market and Financial Implications:**
  - The acquisition's success may depend on partnerships with financial investors.
  - The shipping industry's volatility adds a layer of risk, influencing investor confidence and participation.
