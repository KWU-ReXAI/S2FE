### Summary of Key Economic Ideas and Initiatives:

- **Government Support for SMEs**: The government is actively supporting the international expansion of small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) by facilitating connections between global corporations and domestic venture companies.

- **Korea Startup Center in Seattle**: In August, the Korea Startup Center was established in Seattle to foster collaboration between local startups and global companies, such as Boeing.

- **Collaboration with Boeing**: The partnership with Boeing aims to nurture SMEs in the aerospace sector. This collaboration was highlighted by a pitching event where a Korean mapping data company, Dabeeo, was recognized for its technological and business potential.

- **Role of the Ministry of SMEs and Startups (중소벤처기업부)**: The ministry is playing a mediating role, helping startups overcome financial barriers to international expansion and facilitating their entry into the global value chain.

- **Focus Areas for Development**: The ministry plans to actively cultivate promising SMEs with advanced technologies in aerospace, mobility, AI, smart manufacturing, and avionics.

- **Boeing's Support Program**: Boeing, in collaboration with the ministry, will consider providing cooperation opportunities to selected startups recommended by the ministry and the Korea Institute of Startup & Entrepreneurship Development.

- **Long-term Goals**: The initiative aims to enhance the competitiveness of domestic SMEs in the aerospace sector and expand the ecosystem of collaboration between large and small enterprises globally.
