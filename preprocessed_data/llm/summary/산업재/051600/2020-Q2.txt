### Summary of Key Economic Ideas and Arguments

- **Introduction of S.Sure Program**: 
  - 한전 KPS has initiated the S.Sure program, aimed at supporting small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) in maintaining technical standards.
  
- **Objective**:
  - The program is designed to help SMEs facing management difficulties due to economic downturns, ensuring their technical standards remain stable.
  - It seeks to promote mutual growth between KPS and SMEs.

- **Implementation**:
  - KPS plans to select a variety of companies, including 260 firms specializing in calibration equipment.
  - A total of 15 billion KRW will be allocated to support these companies.

### Logical Structure

1. **Problem Identification**:
   - SMEs are struggling with maintaining technical standards due to economic challenges.

2. **Solution Proposal**:
   - Launch of the S.Sure program by KPS to address these challenges.

3. **Execution Plan**:
   - Selection of diverse companies for support.
   - Financial backing of 15 billion KRW to aid in maintaining technical standards.

4. **Goal**:
   - Achieve mutual growth and stability for both KPS and the participating SMEs.
