#### Company Overview:
- **Foundation and Structure:**
  - LG was established in 1947 and became a holding company in March 2003 through the merger of LGCI and LGEI.
  - In May 2021, LG divided several subsidiaries to form a new holding company, LX Holdings.

- **Business Operations:**
  - LG's subsidiaries operate in various sectors, including IT services (LG CNS), real estate services (DIENO), economic management education and consulting (LG Management Development Institute), and sports (LG Sports).
  - Key business areas include manufacturing broadcast receivers, audio-visual equipment, petrochemical compounds, and telecommunications.

- **Revenue Sources:**
  - Main revenue streams include dividends from subsidiaries, LG brand trademark royalties, and rental income.
