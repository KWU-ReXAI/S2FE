   - South Korea's participation in the "Chip 4 Alliance" with the U.S., Japan, and Taiwan is crucial for semiconductor supply chain cooperation.
   - Lee's role is seen as vital in navigating these international partnerships, especially given the strategic importance of semiconductors.

5. **Stock Market Implications:**
   - Lee's pardon had a modest immediate impact on Samsung Electronics' stock price, which rose by 0.5%.
   - Long-term, his leadership is expected to positively influence the company's stock, potentially reaching previous highs if bold investments are realized.

6. **Financial Performance:**
   - Samsung Electronics is projected to achieve over 300 trillion won in sales this year, a 13% increase from the previous year.
   - Operating profits are expected to exceed 50 trillion won, indicating strong financial health and potential for stock price growth.

7. **Market Outlook:**
   - Despite short-term fluctuations, the long-term outlook for Samsung Electronics is positive, driven by strong operational performance and strategic investments.

The return of Lee Jae-yong to Samsung's management is anticipated to drive significant strategic investments, particularly in semiconductors and batteries, aligning with global trends and legislative incentives.
