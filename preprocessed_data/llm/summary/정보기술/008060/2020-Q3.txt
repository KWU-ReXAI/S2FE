**Summary of Key Economic Ideas and Arguments:**

1. **Market Environment and Investment Opportunities:**
   - The market environment is changing with the election of the U.S. President and discussions around vaccines.
   - Focus on stocks that align with the "Green New Deal" and renewable energy trends.

2. **POSCO Chemical:**
   - POSCO Chemical is highlighted as a promising stock due to its recent capital increase announcement of approximately 1 trillion KRW.
   - The capital will be used for expanding production, particularly in cathode materials (NCMA), indicating rapid market growth.
   - Despite potential short-term stock price adjustments due to the capital increase, the long-term growth potential makes it an attractive investment.
   - The company aims to capture 15-20% of the global market share in cathode and anode materials over the next decade.
   - The stock is considered undervalued compared to its peers, presenting a buying opportunity.

3. **Daeduck Electronics:**
   - Daeduck Electronics is another recommended stock, with a focus on its improving financial performance.
   - The company is transitioning to high-end packaging, which is expected to significantly boost its earnings.
   - Recent investments in new production lines (FCBGA) are expected to drive future growth.
   - A fire at a major competitor's plant (Unimicron in Taiwan) presents a short-term opportunity for Daeduck to gain market share.
   - The stock is currently undervalued, with potential for revaluation as it captures new growth opportunities.

4. **Overall Market and Industry Outlook:**
   - The electric vehicle (EV) market is rapidly growing, with global market share increasing from 3% to 5%.
   - The semiconductor industry, particularly in PCB manufacturing, is closely linked to emerging technologies like autonomous driving and AI, providing strong growth potential.
   - Both POSCO Chemical and Daeduck Electronics are positioned to benefit from these industry trends.

**Conclusion:**
- Both POSCO Chemical and Daeduck Electronics are identified as promising investment opportunities due to their strategic positioning in growing industries and recent developments that enhance their growth prospects.
- Investors are encouraged to consider these stocks as part of a long-term investment strategy, taking advantage of current undervaluation and future growth potential.
