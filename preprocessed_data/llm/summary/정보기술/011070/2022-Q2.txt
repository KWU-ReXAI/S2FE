   - Over the past decade, LG Innotek's revenue has grown from 4 trillion KRW to an expected 18 trillion KRW in the current year.

3. **Stock Market Performance:**
   - The stock price has been attractive during market corrections, reaching a high of 414,500 KRW in 2021 and currently at 349,000 KRW.
   - The company's market capitalization is approximately 8.25 trillion KRW.

   - Despite high debt, the stock price is not considered expensive relative to equity.

6. **Currency Impact:**
   - The company benefits from a strong dollar and weak yen, with potential pre-tax profit increases of 50 billion KRW and 13 billion KRW, respectively, for a 10% change in currency values.

7. **Dependency on Apple:**
   - A significant portion of revenue (73%) comes from Apple, with LG Innotek's products making up 58% of components in iPhones.
   - Apple's influence on LG Innotek's performance is substantial, with historical growth linked to Apple's product launches.

8. **Future Outlook and Concerns:**
   - Growth is expected to slow next year, with revenue and operating profit projections similar to this year.
   - Concerns include reliance on a single customer (Apple) and potential stagnation in growth.
   - Despite short-term challenges, the company is investing in optical solutions and autonomous mobility technology, suggesting potential for long-term innovation and growth.

9. **Conclusion:**
   - While short-term growth may slow, LG Innotek's innovation and strategic investments position it for potential long-term success, akin to a blue-chip stock.
