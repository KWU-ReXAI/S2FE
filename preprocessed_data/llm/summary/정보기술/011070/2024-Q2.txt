3. **Shift in Investment Focus:**
   - As semiconductor stocks lose popularity, investors are turning their attention to MLCC manufacturers like Samsung Electro-Mechanics and LG Innotek.

   - Global MLCC inventory levels have decreased significantly, indicating a recovery in demand.

5. **Currency Fluctuations and Competitive Advantage:**
