#### Copper Foil (동박) Market Overview
- **Definition and Use**: Copper foil, known as "동박" in Korean, is a thin sheet of copper used primarily in printed circuit boards (PCBs) and increasingly in electric vehicle (EV) batteries.
- **Market Characteristics**: The market is driven by the demand for thinner copper foils, which are essential for improving the performance of IT devices and increasing the capacity of EV batteries.
- **Growth Potential**: The copper foil market is expected to grow at an annual rate of 40% over the next five years, driven by the demand for EV batteries.
