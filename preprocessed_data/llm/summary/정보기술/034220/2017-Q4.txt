**Summary of Key Economic Ideas and Arguments:**

1. **Industry Overview:**
   - The display industry, which had a record performance last year, is facing challenges due to falling LCD panel prices.
   - The decline in LCD prices has significantly impacted the first-quarter performance of companies in the industry.

2. **LG Display's Strategic Shift:**
   - LG Display is transitioning its business structure from LCD to OLED to reduce dependency on LCDs, which account for 90% of its revenue.
   - The company aims to enhance profitability by focusing on premium OLED TVs.

3. **Challenges Faced by LG Display:**
   - Despite LG Display's dominance in large OLED panels, it is incurring losses due to low panel prices.
   - The company is expected to record an operating loss in the first quarter for the first time in six years.
   - The potential for turning a profit this year is uncertain.

4. **Market Competition and External Pressures:**
   - Samsung's significant price reduction of its QLED TVs has increased pricing pressure on LG Electronics.
   - LG Display's expansion efforts in China are hindered by delays in government approval for its Guangzhou plant.

5. **Small and Medium OLED Market Issues:**
   - The underperformance of Apple's iPhone X has led to speculation that Apple may reduce its OLED model offerings.
   - LG Display, which was beginning to supply small and medium OLED panels to Apple, faces challenges in securing clients.

6. **Investment and Market Strategy:**
   - LG Display has canceled a 9 billion won order for small and medium OLED equipment, indicating a pause in investment.
   - The company plans to continue investing in OLED, adjusting its pace according to market conditions, but the market outlook remains challenging.

**Cited Examples and Data:**
- Continuous decline in LCD panel prices for 10 months due to competition from Chinese companies.
- LG Display's reliance on LCDs for 90% of its revenue.
- Samsung's 30% price cut on QLED TVs affecting LG Electronics.
- Delays in the construction of LG Display's OLED plant in Guangzhou, China.
- Apple's potential reduction in OLED model offerings impacting LG Display's supply plans.
