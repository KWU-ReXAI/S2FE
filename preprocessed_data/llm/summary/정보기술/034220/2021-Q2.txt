   - OLED technology is advancing from older technologies like CRT, PDP, and LCD.
   - Key advantages of OLED over LCD include thinner displays, no need for backlighting, better contrast with true blacks, faster response times, and lower power consumption.
   - OLED displays are crucial for applications requiring high performance, such as metaverse and autonomous vehicles.

3. **Challenges and Opportunities:**
   - OLED displays are currently more expensive and have a shorter lifespan due to issues like burn-in.
