### Summary of Key Economic Ideas and Arguments:

- **Presidential Pardon and Economic Contributions:**
  - Lee Jae-hyun, Chairman of CJ Group, was pardoned on Liberation Day and expressed his commitment to focus on his health and contribute to the nation and society through business.
  - He apologized for any concerns caused and thanked the president and the public for the opportunity to recover and restart.

- **CJ Group's Future Plans:**
  - CJ Group expressed gratitude for the pardon and committed to striving towards becoming a global cultural enterprise.
  - Despite Lee's health issues, which include a genetic disease affecting his muscles, CJ Group anticipates active management activities within its subsidiaries.

- **Reactions from Other Conglomerates:**
  - Hanwha and SK Group, which had hoped for pardons for their leaders, expressed disappointment.
  - Kim Seung-youn, Chairman of Hanwha, encouraged employees to continue addressing current challenges and committed to his role as a supporter.
  - SK Group noted potential difficulties in pursuing future growth and business initiatives due to the lack of a pardon for Vice Chairman Choi Jae-won but aims to minimize these challenges.
