   - Rising interest rates are significantly impacting SMEs, with many paying between 4% to 6% interest on loans.

2. **Corporate Bond Market Issues:**
   - Both SMEs and large corporations face difficulties in issuing bonds due to a saturated bond market.
   - SK Group, despite its high credit rating, has turned to the long-term CP market for funding, indicating a potential distortion in the bond market.
   - The shift to CP markets by large corporations could crowd out smaller firms and those with lower credit ratings.

3. **SK Group's Financial Moves:**
   - SK Group's entry into the long-term CP market raises concerns about its financial health, especially after its acquisition of Intel's NAND flash memory business.
   - The move suggests potential financial strain and highlights broader issues in the capital market.

4. **Hankook Life Insurance's Dollar Bond Issue:**
   - Hankook Life Insurance faced a crisis by not exercising a call option on its dollar bonds, causing market turmoil.
   - The government and major shareholders intervened to ensure early repayment, highlighting the need for better regulatory oversight.

5. **Project Financing (PF) Loan Risks:**
   - PF loan delinquency rates are rising, particularly in savings banks, posing significant risks to the financial sector.
   - The total PF loans amount to 112.2 trillion won, with a significant portion held by non-bank financial institutions.

6. **Exchange Rate Fluctuations:**
   - The won-dollar exchange rate has fallen below 1,400 won, influenced by expectations of a slowdown in U.S. monetary tightening and China's potential policy shifts.
   - Despite the drop, fundamental economic conditions have not changed significantly, suggesting continued volatility.

7. **China's Export Shock:**
   - China's export growth turned negative in October, raising concerns about a potential economic crisis.
   - The decline in exports and imports indicates a broader global economic slowdown.

8. **Property Tax Resistance:**
   - The number of comprehensive real estate tax payers has surged, leading to increased tax resistance.
   - The government's attempts to adjust tax policies have been blocked by opposition parties, causing frustration among taxpayers.
