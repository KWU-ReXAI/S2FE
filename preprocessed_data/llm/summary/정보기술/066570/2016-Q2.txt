**Summary of Key Economic Ideas and Arguments:**

- **Increased Demand Due to Weather:**
  - The nationwide heatwave has led to a significant increase in demand for air conditioners.

- **Production Adjustment:**
  - LG Electronics is responding to this surge in demand by extending the production period of air conditioners.

- **Operational Changes:**
  - The air conditioner production facilities at LG's Changwon plant will operate for an additional two weeks compared to the previous year, continuing until mid-August.

- **Comparison with Previous Years:**
  - Typically, production would conclude by early August, but the persistent heat this year has necessitated an extension to meet ongoing demand.

**Logical Structure:**

1. **Introduction of Increased Demand:**
   - Explanation of the heatwave's impact on consumer behavior and demand for air conditioners.

2. **Company Response:**
   - Details on LG Electronics' decision to extend production to accommodate increased demand.

3. **Operational Details:**
   - Specifics about the extension of production at the Changwon plant.

4. **Contextual Comparison:**
   - Comparison with past production schedules to highlight the unusual nature of this year's demand.
