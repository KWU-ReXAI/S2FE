   - The remaining funds should be reinvested in high-growth industries that are also currently undervalued.

5. **Investment Recommendations:**
   - Maintain the current position until the stock price reaches 65,000 won, then reduce the holding by 50%.
   - Reallocate the proceeds into high-growth stocks to potentially recover losses over the next few years.
   - The expert emphasizes the importance of adjusting the investment strategy based on market conditions and growth potential.

6. **Conclusion:**
   - The recovery of LG Electronics' stock is contingent on an economic rebound.
   - The expert advises a strategic reduction in holdings during a rebound and reinvestment in growth stocks for better long-term returns.
