**Summary of Key Economic Ideas and Developments in LG Electronics' Robotics Business:**

- **Focus on Robotics as Future Growth Area:**
  - LG Electronics is intensifying its efforts in the robotics sector, identifying it as a key area for future growth.

- **Trademark Applications:**
  - The company has recently filed trademarks for "CLOi Sterilization Bot" and "CLOi Guide Bot," indicating a strategic focus on expanding its robotics brand, CLOi.

- **Types of Robots:**
  - **Sterilization Bot:** Designed for non-contact disinfection tasks to maintain hygiene in specific spaces.
  - **Guide Bot:** Assists consumers with product purchases and provides guidance.

- **Product Launches and Developments:**
  - The Sterilization Bot was first unveiled in December at the Korea Electronics Show.
  - The Guide Bot gained attention during a speech by LG's CTO at the 2019 International Consumer Electronics Show.

- **Expansion of Services:**
  - LG has launched a delivery service using the CLOi ServeBot to transport goods within buildings, showcasing the practical application of its robotics technology.

- **Future Plans:**
  - LG Electronics plans to continue developing robotic service solutions that enhance everyday life, indicating a long-term commitment to innovation in this field.

This summary highlights LG Electronics' strategic initiatives and developments in the robotics industry, emphasizing their commitment to leveraging technology for practical applications and future growth.
