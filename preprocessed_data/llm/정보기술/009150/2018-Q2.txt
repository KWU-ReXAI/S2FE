3. **MLCC Importance:**

   - The demand for MLCCs is driven by the increasing use of electronic components in vehicles, especially with the rise of electric vehicles (EVs).

4. **Market Dynamics:**
   - There is a significant shortage of MLCCs, particularly for automotive applications.
   - Companies like TDK and Murata are shifting production from general-purpose MLCCs to automotive-specific ones, exacerbating the shortage.

5. **Stock Performance and Market Trends:**
   - The stock in question, along with others like Samwha Capacitor, has experienced a decline.
   - The decline is attributed to the easing of shortages in general-purpose MLCCs, leading to market corrections.

6. **Investment Recommendations:**

   - Future investment should be based on the status of the MLCC shortage, particularly in the automotive sector.
   - If shortages persist, there may be opportunities for re-entry into the market after sufficient correction.

7. **Market Sentiment:**
