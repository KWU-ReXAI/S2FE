### Summary of Key Economic Ideas and Arguments:

1. **Patent Registration Rankings:**
   - The United States Patent and Trademark Office released a list of top companies for patent registrations in 2018.
   - Samsung Electronics and LG Electronics from South Korea ranked in the top 10, with Samsung maintaining the 2nd position for 12 consecutive years and LG in the top 10 for three years.
   - The list includes six American companies, two South Korean companies, and one each from Taiwan and Japan.
   - IBM from the United States holds the 1st position, while Apple is ranked 11th.
   - A notable shift is observed as Japan, which had six companies in the top 10 a decade ago, now has only Canon remaining.

2. **Russian Government and YouTube:**
   - The Russian government requested Google to block illegal protest promotions on YouTube.
   - Large-scale protests have been occurring in Moscow, demanding fair elections after opposition candidates were barred from the upcoming city council elections.
   - The Russian government accuses Western powers of interfering in its political processes through media and the internet.

3. **Legal Ruling on Mandatory Service for Pilots:**
   - A court ruled that a mandatory 10-year service agreement for a maritime police pilot was invalid.
   - The pilot, who served for over four years, was sued for education cost reimbursement after leaving the service.
   - The court found the service agreement unlawful, as it exceeded the training period and restricted the individual's career freedom without legal backing.

4. **Weather Impact on Maritime Operations:**
   - Typhoon Lekima affected maritime operations along China's east coast, impacting passenger ferry services between Incheon and nearby islands.
   - Severe weather warnings, including high waves and strong winds, led to the suspension of ferry operations.

5. **Sports Update:**
   - Ryu Hyun-jin of the LA Dodgers achieved his 12th win of the season after returning from an injury.
   - He pitched seven innings without allowing a run, contributing to the Dodgers' 9-3 victory.
   - Ryu reached a career milestone of 150 wins across his professional career in both Korea and the United States.
